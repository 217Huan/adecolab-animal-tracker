Generic Motion TrackerBrowser-based motion analysis for animal behaviour studies

Pipeline

  • 1. Upload video
  • 2. Apply contrast and blur filters
  • 3. Compare current frame to background
  • 4. Find moving centroid
  • 5. Build heatmap and chart

Supported use

  • Single moving subject in a mostly static scene
  • Fish, insects, rodents, or other animals
  • Works best with fixed camera and stable lighting
